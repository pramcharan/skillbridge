Privacy Policy

How SkillBridge collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, complete your profile, post jobs, submit proposals, or communicate through our platform.

  • Account data: name, email address, password (hashed), profile photo
  • Profile data: skills, bio, location, portfolio items, hourly rate
  • Activity data: jobs posted, proposals submitted, projects completed, reviews given
  • Communications: messages sent through project chat and community rooms
  • OAuth data: if you connect Google or GitHub, we receive your public profile information from those providers
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, usage patterns
2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve SkillBridge:

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Matching freelancers with relevant job opportunities using our AI scoring system
  • Processing transactions and managing project workflows
  • Sending notifications about account activity, proposals, and messages
  • Sending password reset and security emails
  • Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Improving our AI models and platform features
  • Complying with legal obligations
3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Other users: your public profile, portfolio, and reviews are visible to other platform members
  • Service providers: trusted third parties who assist in operating our platform (e.g. Cloudinary for file storage, email service providers)
  • Legal requirements: when required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
4. Cookies & Local Storage

SkillBridge uses browser localStorage to store your authentication token for session management. We do not use third-party tracking cookies. You may clear your browser storage at any time, which will log you out of the platform.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal information is anonymised within 24 hours — your name becomes "Deleted User" and your email address is replaced with an anonymised identifier. Content you have created (job posts, reviews, messages) may remain on the platform in anonymised form.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are hashed using BCrypt — we never store plaintext passwords
  • Authentication uses JWT tokens with HS512 signing
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • File uploads are processed and stored via Cloudinary with access controls
  • Revoked tokens are tracked to prevent reuse after logout

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no security system is impenetrable. Please use a strong, unique password and keep it confidential.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: update inaccurate information via your profile settings
  • Deletion: delete your account via the Danger Zone in your profile settings
  • Portability: request an export of your data
  • Objection: object to certain types of processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@skillbridge.dev.

8. Children's Privacy

SkillBridge is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you via email or a prominent notice on the platform at least 14 days before the changes take effect. Your continued use of SkillBridge after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
